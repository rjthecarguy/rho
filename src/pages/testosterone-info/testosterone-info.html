Testosterone Information

Testosterone is the primary sex hormone in men. Many men are diagnosed with low testosterone or hypogonadism every year. Symptoms of low testosterone include: fatigue, weight gain, erectile dysfunction, low libido, moodiness, loss of enjoyment in life, and depression.
